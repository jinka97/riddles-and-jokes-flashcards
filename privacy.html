Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Welcome to Bolty & Chatty's Flashcard Fun. We respect your privacy and want to help you understand how we collect and use information.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to gather anonymous usage data such as device, browser, and page views. No personally identifiable info is collected.

Local Storage

Your preferences and scores are stored locally in your browser using `localStorage`. This data is never sent to a server.

Cookies

We use cookies only to remember your consent preferences and enable analytics (if accepted).

Security

We don’t store sensitive data or use any third-party advertising networks.

For Children

This app is designed to be safe and does not knowingly collect information from users under age 13.
