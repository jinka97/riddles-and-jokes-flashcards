Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Bolty & Chatty Flashcard Fun. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data, such as page views and interactions, to improve our app. This may include IP address, device type, and browser data.

Google Analytics uses cookies to gather data anonymously. This data is not personally identifiable and is only used to analyze traffic and improve our content.

Read more at Google Privacy Policy or opt out here.

Locally Stored Data

We store game preferences and scores in your browser using localStorage. This includes:

  • Your score (correct/incorrect)
  • Muted sound preferences
  • Selected categories and decks

This data stays in your browser and is not sent to us or any third party.

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have unintentionally collected such data, please contact us.

Changes

We may update this policy periodically. Please check this page regularly for updates.

Contact

For questions or concerns, reach us at: support@boltychattyflashcards.com
